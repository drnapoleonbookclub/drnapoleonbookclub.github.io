Discussion is hosted by Disqus, you may log in with your Twitter account. Members shall use their real names.

Choose a topic from the site index or propose a new topic at general discussion. Topics shall indicate which of Dr Napoleon's works introduce the concept and readers should be familiar with the entirety of that book/white paper before commenting.

Book Club

It may be worthwhile to choose a title to re-read as a group, with a weekly discussion on each chapter. Indicate interest in the general discussion.
